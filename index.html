About MEGLO

MEGLO is not just a car brand. It's a revolution in motion. Born from bold dreams and fueled by unshakable ambition, MEGLO blends cutting-edge hybrid engineering with luxurious design and futuristic performance. Founded by visionaries Bakhatla Mohasi and Katleho Tshabalala, MEGLO aims to break barriers and put Africa on the global automotive innovation map.

The Dream

Driven by a passion to create the most beautiful and powerful cars ever imagined, MEGLO is designing super-hybrids that compete with the fastest in the world. With performance stats that defy expectations, MEGLO vehicles are created to inspire awe — and pride.

Meet the Founders

Bakhatla Mohasi and Katleho Tshabalala are two innovative minds with a shared mission: to disrupt the status quo and introduce a new era of automotive design from the heart of Africa. Their vision, determination, and unique style set MEGLO apart from all competitors.
